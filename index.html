The Paris Olympic Games 2024 is hold in Paris, France, from July 26 to August 11, 2024. This is the third time Paris has hosted the Olympics Game. The game have 32 sports and 329 events. There are over 10,000 athletes around the world compete in the games. This website will show a little bit infomation about the game.
